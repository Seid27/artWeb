Learning

The Art Bar offers relaxed, informal painting classes that are fun for the whole family. We have a variety of paintings that we teach using Acrylic paints. It's a place where you can let your creativity flow onto the canvas!

Private Parties

The Art Bar will be there to make it amazing. We do require a $50 deposit to hold your date. Prices vary depending on the painting you choose for your party. Outside beverages are not allowed, but you may bring your own food for the party.

Open Paintings

Open painting is for anyone, you be the artist. The pricing differs based on the size of your canvas. Everything is still provided, including paints, brushes, and aprons. Facilitators will be there to offer limited instruction. They answer questions, and make suggestions, but do not provide traditional lessons during open painting time.

See calendar for open painting days, and times.

Times and days will vary.

Painting with Pets

Bring In your fur babies and enjoy a day of painting. Please call to schedule an appointment.

What is The Art Painting Party like?

The Art Bar Painting Party is a fun for all ages In an upbeat social setting, our instructors will guide you step by step, in recreating the nights featured painting. Event times vary, and we do recommend coming at least 20-30 minutes early to pick your seats, paint, and drinks. We offer your choice of beer, wine, soda, or water.
